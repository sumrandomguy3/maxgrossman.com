Hand Carved Spoon

Each Spoon is hand carved and crafted from domestically sourced Walnut, Cherry, or Maple. They are lovingly crafted and shaped to fit ergonomically into your hands. They are finished in a bath of mineral oil and coated in a layer of bees wax to protect them from boiling pots of water and sauce. These spoons will make cooking a joy for years to come! An annual coating of mineral oil and bees wax will keep these spoons in top shape for years to come! They can be washed with hot soapy water.
