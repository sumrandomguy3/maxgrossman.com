About

Hi, I'm Max — and yes, I really do make my own sawdust.

I'm a furniture designer/maker and a graduate of the Rochester Institute of Technology's School of American Crafts, working in wood and metal out of West Orange, New Jersey. My work has been shown at the 2014 CIAS Honors Show as well as Gallery R and the Vignelli Center in Rochester, NY. I also teach the next generation as the woodshop teacher at West Orange High School.

My work focuses on dynamic pieces that mix wood and metal, drawing on the industrial machinery of modern life — with a healthy dislike for right angles and a soft spot for asymmetry, curves and clever joinery. I like going against the tide of disposable "flat pack" furniture, building pieces that are comfortable, sustainable, and made to be treasured for years.

On top of furniture I design and build custom built-ins and details that set a home apart. Always up for a challenge — if you've got a project, I'd love to hear about it.
